who we are

two indie game devs from switzerland, driven by passion and a shared vision: to push forward the genres we love but feel are often orverlooked, and to create the kind of games we've always wanted to play.

how we do it

we love handcrafted stuff — and that's how we build our games. every asset you see, from models and textures to animations, is created by ourselves. we focus on adding a personal touch to every detail, making our games feel unique, authentic, and full of care.

our philosophy

we believe in quality over quantity. every choice we make - from mechanics to level design - is guided by our desire to create something that truly stands out.
we'd rather spend ten years making one game that feels unique, than rush out ten that all feel the same.
